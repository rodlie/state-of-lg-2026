# Sample Project

Sample project is a demonstration to give an example of how projects can be submitted for the State of Libre Graphics 2026.

### Further Links:
https://libregraphicsmeeting.org

## Slide 0 - title slide
![title slide](./slide0.png)
Every year, the [Libre Graphics Meeting](https://libregraphicsmeeting.org) starts with an opening presentation called the **State of Libre Graphics**, an update from the many software, curation publication and umbrella projects of our Libre Graphics community. To help, we made a the SampleProject! So let's see what SampleTeam has been up to this year!

## Slide 1 - Changelog
![slide 1](./slide1.png)
We love to see what projects have been up to! Last year, we had a great conference with 19 projects that submitted slides to the State of Libre Graphics. We encourage following best slide practices of having more visual imagery on the slides, and less text. Whatever you put into here will be read.

Some cool things that happened last year so I can put an example bullet list here and try and encourage you to come:
- Made new friends!
- Learned interesting things at talks.
- Participated in some workshops. I learned some new things about rendering in Blender and learned how to performe UX testing.
- Went on a photo walk across Nuremberg with others.

Submitting to the State of LibreGraphics allows all the projects to give updates at the conference, without having to reserve a whole 20 min talk for every project just to talk about what they've been up to.

## Slide 2 - Roadmap
![slide 2](./slide2.png)
Where is your project going next? The future is always a fun place to make new things!

A few more suggestions for submitting slides:
- Make your slide in the software your presenting about! It's always so cool to see all the amazing things your software can create.
- Don't write whole essays in the text here. We have quite a bit of projects to get through unfortunately. 
- Make sure your slides are also readable. Don't use white font on a white background :).

We look forward to your submissions! Thanks for submitting! And hope to see you at LGM if you can come!
